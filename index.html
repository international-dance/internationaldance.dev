Classes

Classes are held in Annandale VA and are available for ages three years to adult. Classes run from 30 minutes to 2 hours.

Schedule

Performances

Performances are held each spring for our annual recital. In addition to the Spring Recital, we carry on the tradition of our International Christmas Performance, which has delighted audiences for years. We also perform at the Green Spring Nursing Home throughout the year, spreading "happiness through dancing."

Schedule

History

International Dance carries on the long and distinguished legacy of the former English Royal Ballet Soloist and The School of International Dance Founder Christina Heimlich (1918 - 2012). Thousands of dancers have benefited over the years from her unique and matchless style and discipline as they will continue on into the future.
